  • Match + Village Apparel

    We get to choose what brand we wear for in game matches. In 2021 Tokyo, Phil Dalhausser wore Slunks for shirt and shorts, not sure what brand for his hat. However, for opening and closing ceremonies, as well as podium pictures, I think we have to wear USOPC clothing, which I think is Mizuno for USAV. Any specific questions or obligations we'd like to √√ with the USAV director. I attached a photo of what USOPC clothing means.

    Additionally, if we are able to land any deals, in order to wear them around the village and (I think?) in matches, they need to fill out a Rule 40 application. It looks pretty straight forward and easy to get approved but I just got notified about it and didn't look super deep in it.

    They allow a 10 cm^2 manufacturer logo on hats (or two 5 cm^2), 30 cm^2 manufacturer logo on tank top, 30 cm^2 on shorts. From my understanding, the player number must be on shirt, shorts, and hat. The word USA must be on hat and shirt, not shorts. No logos of anything else may be allowed. We plan to wear logos of our other sponsors as much as possible outside of competition whenever allowed, but in competition we can only represent manufacturer logos. The uniform guidelines for 2024 are attached to this email where you can see more details.
